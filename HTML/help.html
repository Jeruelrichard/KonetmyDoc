FAQ

How do i book an appointment?

First login into your account or create a new account if you don't have one already, you will be directed to the Home page.

On the top right corner of the home page is the Book Appointment, click it and you will be able to quickly book appointment.

Can i chat with a doctor online?

No you can not. All interactions with your doctor will be done in person.

How can i access my test results?

All test results will be made available in your documents which is located in your dashboard.

Is my health data secured?

Yes abosultely, all information in our database is protected by our Privacy Policy.

What should i do in case of an emergency?

All emergency cases are given priority and so you can book an appointment as soon as possoble.
